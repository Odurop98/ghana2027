About the Conference

A residential gathering where memory meets responsibility.

Ghana 2027 convenes scholars, practitioners, consultants, students and leaders from across Africa and the diaspora to think rigorously and feel deeply about reparations, historical memory, leadership and authority — and the institutions we are responsible for repairing.

This is not a tourism programme, an activist campaign, or a purely academic conference. It is a serious, reflective space for dialogue and future-making, rooted in Ghana and connected to the global African diaspora.
